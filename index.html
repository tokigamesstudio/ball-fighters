How to Play

  1. Pick your fighter
  2. Set your stake (£0.10 – £500)
  3. Hit FIGHT — your opponent is randomly assigned
  4. Watch the battle play out
  5. Win your stake × odds if your fighter wins

Provably Fair

Every fight outcome is determined by a cryptographic seed committed before your bet. After the fight, the seed is revealed — you can verify the result independently using SHA-256.
