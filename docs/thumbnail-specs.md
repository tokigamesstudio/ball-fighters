# Game Thumbnail Specs (Stake Engine)

## Requirements

- **Bright overall** — must not clash with Stake's dark background
- **No dark edges** — avoid dark borders that blend into the platform
- **Background**: Bright, appropriate for the game theme
- **Foreground**: Key focus area correctly filled (fighter imagery)
- **Gradient**: Similar colour to the background
- **Title**: Fits within inner guidelines, not too close to edges
- **No text/multipliers** on background or foreground image

## Recommended Design

### Background
- Arena floor with energy glow (purple/blue gradient, bright center)
- Subtle particle effects suggesting combat

### Foreground
- 2-3 fighter silhouettes in action poses (balls with elemental effects)
- Fire (orange), Electric (gold), Earth (brown), Shadow (purple)

### Title
- "BALL FIGHTERS" or "ARENA FIGHTERS"
- Centered, bold, white with subtle glow
- Keep within 80% of tile width

### Gradient
- Purple-to-blue matching the arena background

## Dimensions

Check Stake Engine's current tile specs (typically 280×380px or similar aspect ratio). The tile appears in their game lobby grid.
